Neuronal Network Activity Analysis Across Development and Pharmacological Conditions

Academic Project | Neuroengineering | 2026

Analysis of human pluripotent stem cell-derived neuronal networks cultured on microelectrode arrays, focused on comparing spontaneous development with pharmacologically induced activity patterns.

  • Developed a Python-based pipeline for MEA signal processing and neuronal activity analysis.
  • Applied filtering, spike detection, burst detection, and network-level feature extraction.
  • Analyzed firing rate, mean burst duration, and spikes per burst across DIV 3–35.
  • Compared developmental activity with pharmacological conditions such as GABA, Gabazine, CNQX, D-AP5, Kainic Acid, and TTX.
  • Created similarity matrices to evaluate whether drug-induced states resembled developmental network stages.
